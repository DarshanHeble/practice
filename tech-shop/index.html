SUPPORT & COMPATIBILITY

Find answers about delivery, device compatibility, and warranty policies.

Do these cases support Qi2 and MagSafe wireless charging?

Yes. Every case is fitted with N52 neodymium magnetic alignment rings. They snap automatically to Apple MagSafe stands, magnetic wallet sleeves, and Qi2 wireless chargers at maximum rates.

What materials are integrated inside the Carbon cases?

We combine aerospace-grade real carbon-fiber weaves with high-impact TPU internal linings. This dual-layered composition deflects impacts from drop heights while cushioning the back glass of the phone.

How fast is order processing and standard shipment?

Orders are processed and packed within 24 hours. Transit speeds take 2-4 business days for domestic regions, and 5-9 days for international shipments. Tracking links are emailed immediately upon courier collection.
