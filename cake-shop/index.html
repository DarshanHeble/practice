THE ART OF MAKING BEAUTIFUL CAKES

At L'Amour du Sucre, we believe that celebration centerpieces should taste as stunning as they look. We started as a small kitchen studio, and have evolved into the city's premier pastry shop.

We use only farm-fresh fresh eggs, imported Belgian chocolate, fresh lavender, and local berries. We bake everything slowly, in small batches, to guarantee soft layers and rich taste.

Our Artistry

THE PATH TO PERFECTION

Crafting custom cakes requires patience, precision, and artistry. Here is how your dream centerpiece comes to life.

I

Consultation

We sketch your concepts, match color swatches, and choose custom flavor profiles during tasting.

II

Sourcing

We gather farm-fresh organic ingredients, fresh flowers, and premium dark cocoa butter.

III

Slow Baking

Layers are baked slowly in small batches to guarantee softness and great taste.

IV

Hand Decoration

Our head pastry chef completes icing, piping, sugar modeling, and applies real edible 24k gold leaf.
