Our Heritage

Crafting Pure Vegetarian Legacies Since 2012

"Vegetarianism is not a lack of choices; it is the art of celebrating nature's endless flavor canvas."

Tastes & More was born out of a simple passion: to elevate traditional Indian vegetarian food to a premium dining experience. We felt that vegetarians deserved a space that combined absolute culinary authenticity with luxury aesthetics, warm hospitality, and generous portions.

For over a decade, we have remained committed to this standard. We grind our own spice blends daily, churn our own butter, source organic produce, and refuse to use chemical additives, artificial colors, or MSG. Every plate we serve represents our dedication to your health and taste.
